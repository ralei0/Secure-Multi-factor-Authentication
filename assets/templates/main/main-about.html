About

ShieldVerify


Our platform provides a secure and user-friendly two-factor authentication (2FA) solution for our users. We understand the importance of keeping your personal information safe and secure, and we strive to provide the highest level of security for our users. Our 2FA solution includes two methods of authentication - OTP (One-Time Password) and graphical password. With OTP, a unique code is sent to the user's registered mobile number, which must be entered along with their username and password during login. This helps to ensure that only authorized users have access to their accounts. In addition, we also offer graphical password authentication, which provides an extra layer of security. With this method, users must select four specific locations on a pre-selected picture during account setup. These locations are then used as the user's graphical password, and must be selected in the same order during login. This method ensures that even if someone obtains the user's username and password, they still cannot access the account without selecting the correct locations. At our platform, we are committed to providing our users with the highest level of security for their accounts, and we are constantly working to improve and update our security measures to stay ahead of the latest threats.
